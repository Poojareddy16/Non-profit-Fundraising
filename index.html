Non-profit Fundraiser

Our crowdfunding platform charges NO fees

Starting a fundraiser is easy, as anyone can start a DIY (Do-It-Yourself) campaign. The campaign organizers are required to sign up and enter basic information about the beneficiary - the person/group/NGO for whom the funds are raised. Enter details about the campaign (such as the story/cause) and required fund amount along with the timeline by which the funds are required.
